## 基本初等函数的导数公式

- $c' = 0$
- $(x^n)' = nx^{n-1}$
- $(\sin x)' = \cos x$
- $(\cos x)' = -\sin x$
- $(a^x)' = a^x\ln a  \kern2px (a\gt 0)$
- $(e^x)' = e^x$
- $(\log_ax)' = \frac 1{x\ln a}  \kern2px (a \gt 0, a\ne 1)$
- $(\ln x)' = \frac 1 x$

## 导数运算法则

- $[f(x)\pm g(x)]' = f'(x)\pm g'(x)$
- $[f(x) \cdot g(x)]' = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)$
- $[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}]' = \frac{f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{[g(x)]^2} \kern2px (g(x) \ne 0)$
- $[f(g(x))]' = f'_gg'x$
